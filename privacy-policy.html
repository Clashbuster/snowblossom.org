Snowblossom Privacy Policy

The scope of this privacy policy is the Android App Snowblossom

The app uses the camera only for the purpose of capturing QR codes to send to when requested by the user.

The app stores wallet data locally and never transmits it anywhere with the exception of protocol requests sent to Snowblossom servers.

The information sent to the servers consists of the following things

  • Addresses in the wallet - for the purpose of getting balance and history
  • Transactions to send, when sending funds
